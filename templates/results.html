The flute is a wind instrument and the most sensitive to tuning as it has the mouthpiece outside the mouth (compared to other woodwind instruments). Posture must be stable and safe.
A trick to improve the tuning in the performance is: When you feel or see in the tuner that the note is low you can correct it by placing the embouchure "down" as much as you feel or see the pitch improve; otherwise, when the pitch is higher, you move the embouchure "out" of your mouth to correct it.
